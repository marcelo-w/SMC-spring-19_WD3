Black Hats

Hat Fact: Rarely seen without his stovepipe hat, President Abraham Lincoln helped make the black top hat popular.

Balaclava

Balaclavas are used in outdoor winter sports to protect the face from wind and maintain warmth. They are also popular among motorcyclists and bicyclists.

Leather Cap

The black leather flat cap is often combined with a leather jacket and dark clothes in popular culture to depict a burglar, mugger, or robber, occasionally with a domino mask.

Fedora

The women's rights movement adopted the fedora as a fashionable symbol. Popular among men for its stylishness and its ability to protect the wearer's head from the weather.

Cowboy Hat

Few white cowboy hats are worn in American Westerns, either by heroes or villains. Imagine how dirty a white hat becomes in a dusty environment.
