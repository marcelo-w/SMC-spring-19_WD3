White Hats

Hat Fact: The most famous white-hat-wearing cowboy, the Lone Ranger, first rode onto the screen in 1938.

Beanie

Knitted caps are common in cold climates, and are worn worldwide in various forms. They have become the common headgear for stereotypical dockworkers and sailors in movies and television.

Baseball Hat

A part of the traditional baseball uniform, and worn by players with the brim pointing forward to shield the eyes from the sun. The baseball cap has become a common fashion accessory.

Flat Cap

The flat cap is sometimes associated with older men, skinheads, the Oi!, punk, and hip hop subcultures, sometimes worn back-to-front or cocked to the side.

Bucket Hat

Traditionally worn by Irish farmers and fishermen as protection from the rain. These lightweight hats are popular for use in sports such as fishing, and as sun protection.
